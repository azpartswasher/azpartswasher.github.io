WHAT ELSE CAN I DO?
There are lots of simple ways to go a little greener every day. Check out our tips below ...
• Make purchases responsibly. Search online to find out which brands and retailers work to protect the environment
• Buy locally produced food. Local businesses don't add to pollution with extensive shipping, and everything is fresher and tastier!
• Reduce your trash. Re-use items when possible. Choose new items with less packaging. Avoid disposable napkins, dishes, and plastic silverware. Donate your "junk" - someone else will treasure it!
• Become more efficient. Reduce your water usage with low-flower showerheads. Reduce electricity usage with compact fluorescent light bulbs. Walk short distances instead of driving.
